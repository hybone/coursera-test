Celebraties in sports

Basketball--Micheal Jordan

In fields of green and courts of clay, Where athletes strive and children play, The spirit of the game takes flight, In morning sun and fading light.

Soccer-- Robin van Persie

With every pass and every goal, The heart of sports begins to roll, A dance of skill, a test of might, In every match, a new delight.

100 meters dash-- Usain St Leo Bolt

From cricket pitch to basketball, In every sport, we give our all, The cheers, the tears, the highs, the lows, In every game, the passion shows.
